ABOUT FLAVOUR.sa

Flavour.sa was founded in 2020 as we experienced lockdown
many entertainment spaces that require big crowds were shutdown.
As restrictions started going down people were no longer used the idea of going out.
This then drove us , a group of creatives to become innovate through the power of social media and the internet
We work hand in hand with entertainment businesses by promoting them and drawing in consumers.
People who accessed these events via our website were reward tickets , insight and other exciting information about upcoming events


OUR VISION & MISSION
There is plans underway about expansion to the company ,We want to venture into charitable work
there are many artists and creatives in South Africa who are not give the correct platforms to showcase
We are now in talks with event companies to incorporate events the are focused on the the exposure and promotion of even those
creatives that are not known by people.This will help them connect with artists with bigger names ,and it will open up the space
for South Africa's heritage and art to be show cased with the right promotion we could expand to the world.

Founder: Mashadi Kgomo
